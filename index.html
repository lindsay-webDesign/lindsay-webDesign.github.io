My Poem

By Lindsay Whitman-Orlin

I went outside and
saw a little fox

The fox jumped into a box

I asked him where his mama had gone

The fox said "I dont know, she was just
on the lawn!"

I told the fox Id wait with him
for his mother to return

We heard some rustling in the leaves from a nearby fern

"Maybe that's your mom." I told the fox

The little fox was was so excited, he jumped out of his box

Mama fox crawled out of the fern to reunite with her little fox

The little fox went on to follow his mother and
went to the lake to eat some lox
